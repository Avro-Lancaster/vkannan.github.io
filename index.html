Projects

Selected work in aerospace, simulation, and applied AI.

Finite Wing Aerodynamics Analysis

Automated AVL simulations to evaluate effects of aspect ratio, taper, sweep, dihedral, and winglets on lift distribution, induced drag, and efficiency. Produced parametric studies with >40 configurations.

Vortex Lattice Method Solver

Developed a MATLAB VLM solver with vortex ring elements to replicate classical results (Jacobs, 1947). Implemented custom meshing functions and verified against AVL.

PC-9 Flight Simulator

Built a full nonlinear MATLAB flight simulator for the PC-9 trainer aircraft. Simulated aerobatic manoeuvres and control responses with trim, propulsion, and aerodynamic models.

UCAV Design Project

Led aerodynamic and propulsion analysis for a long-endurance UAV with biplane wing configuration. Work included performance modelling, stability analysis, and advanced technology assessment.

ML for Crack Propagation in Composites

Developed a Python tool using CV + neural networks to automatically calculate crack growth rates from DCB test video frames. Applied to graphene-enhanced fibre-reinforced composites.

Tello Drone Precision Landing

Integrated ORB feature detection and a PID controller in Python + OpenCV for autonomous detection and landing on mission pads with the Tello drone.
